We are a Tanzania-based manufacturer and supplier of uPVC Windows, Doors and Partition Solutions, serving residential, commercial and institutional developments across East and Central Africa. Our products are designed to suit local climatic conditions, modern architectural requirements and the growing demand for secure, long lasting performance, energy-efficient and lowmaintenance building solutions. From high-end luxury homes and hotels to offices, apartments and public buildings, we deliver uPVC systems that combine strength, functionality and refined aesthetics to meet the needs of both luxury developments and everyday residential and commercial projects. Through advanced manufacturing processes, skilled craftsmanship and customer-focused service, we provide uPVC solutions that enhance spaces, improve comfort, and add lasting value.
